What are Constraints ?

In MySQL, constraints are rules and conditions that you can apply to database tables to ensure the data stored in the tables follows specific criteria and maintains data integrity. Constraints help enforce business rules, prevent invalid or inconsistent data, and ensure the accuracy and reliability of the database
